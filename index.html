About me

Hey there, so as you might have guessed due to the in your face heading, my name is in fact Shane Howe. I am an aspiring software engineer hailing from Co. Kerry in Ireland. I am studying Computing with Software Development in Munster Technological University (MTU), Kerry.

What made me want to go down this career path? Well as cliche as it sounds, it started with the classic "Hello, World!" program. I had some free time and decided you know what, I am going to learn how to code. I jumped on my laptop rearching where I can learn for free. I stumbled across Codecademy's learn Python3 course. Now bear in mind I had no idea what code even looked like. I loved it. Time passed and I heard about CS50x Harvard's introduction to Computer Science. Best decision I ever made was taking that course. It really set in stone that this was the career path for me. Since then I have gone onto learn Python (more in depth), JavaScript and of course HTML & CSS. Currently dipping my toes into C++.

Outside of all the techy stuff, I am a pretty normal (so I think) guy. I enjoy going to the gym, cooking, reading and socialising. Well if you made it this far, thank you for reading!
